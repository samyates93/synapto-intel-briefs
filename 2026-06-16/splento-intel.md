## Payment flow

**Corporate clients** (Google, Visa, Facebook, Accenture, Ford, KPMG) → **Splento + Synapto** → **Photographers** (1,500+ vetted freelancers, 50+ countries)

- Pays in: B2B
- Beneficiary type: Freelance photographers and videographers (1,500+ vetted, global, 50+ countries)
- Float window: Corporate clients (Google, Visa, Facebook, Accenture, KPMG) pay on standard B2B invoice terms (30-60 days typical). Photographers paid weekly after shoots. Float window 30-60 days creates working capital requirement proportional to booking volume.
