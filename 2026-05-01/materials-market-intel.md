## Payment flow

**Trade Buyers and Contractors** (B2B trade credit (30-day EOM) and B2C card) → **Materials Market + Synapto** → **Supplier branches** (750+ UK merchant branches)

- Pays in: Both
- Payouts: Per-order commission settlement to 750+ supplier branches
- Beneficiary type: Building material supplier branches
- Float window: Trade credit buyers pay 30 days from end of the month of order (effective 30-60 days from order date). Materials Market must settle with suppliers on delivery or shortly after, creating a sustained float gap.
