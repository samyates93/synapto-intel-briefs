## PRQ preliminary findings

**Marketplace language in product**

Shepper website states: 'Dynamic tasks and questionnaires can be customised and launched into a live marketplace.' This is the language of a two-sided marketplace connecting task providers with workers.

**Principal contractor framing in T&Cs**

Terms of service state: 'Shepper shall perform the Service with reasonable care and skill' and Shepherds are 'employed or engaged by Shepper'. Customers cannot 'request the performance of the Service from Shepherds directly other than through Shepper'. This positions Shepper as principal, not intermediary.

**Platform wallet with weekly withdrawal**

Become-a-Shepherd FAQ: 'Get paid quickly and directly into your chosen bank account via weekly withdrawals from your Shepper wallet.' Platform holds funds between brand payment and Shepherd disbursement.

**No Stripe Connect signals**

No Stripe Connect or third-party payment processor named in public-facing documents or privacy policy.
