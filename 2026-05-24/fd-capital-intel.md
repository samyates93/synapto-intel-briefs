## Payment flow

**Client** (VC/PE-backed businesses, SMEs, financial services firms paying placement fees) → **FD Capital + Synapto** → **Interim CFO/FD** (PSC directors (gross payments), umbrella company workers)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Gross PSC payments + umbrella funding for interim contractor book
- Beneficiary type: Interim and fractional CFOs / Finance Directors
- Float window: Client invoiced on 30-60 day terms; interim contractor paid weekly or fortnightly. Gap between contractor payment and client receipt is the float window. At 10 active interims at £1,000/day, represents ~£100k+ monthly flow.
