## Payment flow

**Client** (Digital agencies, tech vendors, martech/adtech brands, media owners, publishers) → **Sphere Digital Recruitment + Synapto** → **Contract Specialist** (Marketing, data analytics, product, adtech, ad ops, social media contractors)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Weekly/fortnightly contractor payments across PSC and umbrella routes
- Beneficiary type: Digital contract workers
- Float window: Digital/martech client invoices on 30-60 day terms; contractor payments weekly. With 200+ active contracts at typical martech/adtech day rates (£300-£800/day), the float window represents a substantial ongoing cash obligation. Trade debtors £1.9m at year-end confirms scale.
