## Payment flow

**Client** (Public sector, housing associations, private sector legal/finance/HR) → **Sellick Partnership + Synapto** → **Contract Worker** (PAYE employees, umbrella company workers, PSC Ltd company directors)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Weekly PAYE payroll + umbrella company funding + gross PSC payments
- Beneficiary type: Contract workers
- Float window: Classic staffing float: client invoiced on 30-60 day terms; worker paid weekly. Gap funded from cash reserves or revolving credit. Trade debtors of £20.6m at year-end confirm scale of float.
