## PRQ preliminary findings

**Operator-MoR confirmed via press ('single payment from customer')**

Mirakl case study and press coverage confirm Turner Price collects a single payment from the caterer for both 1P and 3P orders, then takes commission on 3P sales. Classic marketplace operator-MoR pattern.

**UK's first B2B foodservice marketplace (Mirakl-powered, scaling rapidly)**

Launched with 4,500 products; grown to 12,000+ SKUs; targeting 50,000. 'Record website traffic' reported post-launch. Online sales ~50% of total according to discovery source.

**Revenue £97.22m (FY March 2024, +11% YoY)**

Filed accounts (via GlobalDatabase from CH filings). Company was 'on track for £100m' per prior year. £97m is the most precise confirmed figure available.

**Acquired by Bidcorp UK (Caterfood) July 2024**

Acquisition confirmed July 1 2024. Turner Price becomes 8th member of Caterfood Buying Group. Operates as autonomous independent business. Andrew Selley (Bidcorp UK CEO) is a director.

**3P seller cohort growing as marketplace scales**

Range grew from 4,500 (1P only) to 12,000+ including 3P. 3P sellers require settlement/payout infrastructure. Mirakl Payout module exists but its deployment at Turner Price is unconfirmed.

## Outreach angles

### Primary, Managing Director / COO

**Marketplace seller payout automation as the marketplace scales to 50,000 SKUs**

You've launched the UK's first B2B foodservice marketplace and you're heading to 50,000 SKUs. Every new 3P seller means a new settlement run. Synapto Flow automates multi-seller commission netting and payout at scale -- it's the operational infrastructure your marketplace needs before manual settlement becomes a bottleneck.

### Secondary, Caterfood MD (Philip Atyeo)

**Group-level marketplace payout infrastructure across 8 Caterfood members**

Turner Price is the proof-of-concept for Caterfood's marketplace model. If you're rolling the platform across other members, Synapto Flow becomes the group payout layer -- one integration, consistent settlement, consolidated reporting -- rather than each member managing their own seller payouts independently.

### Tertiary, CFO / Finance Director

**Triver advance on the B2B caterer debtor book**

With 2,500 caterer and restaurant accounts on credit terms and £97m of annual throughput, the debtor book is your largest balance sheet asset. Triver can advance against those invoices at competitive rates -- worth benchmarking even if Barclays already provides a facility.
