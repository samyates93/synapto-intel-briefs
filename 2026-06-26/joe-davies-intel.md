## Outreach angles

### Primary, Finance Director

**Triver to optimise (or replace) the RBS Invoice Finance facility**

You took on RBS Invoice Finance to fund the EOT transition in January 2024 -- that cost sits on top of the NatWest facility and the vendor loan note. Triver advances against your indie retailer invoices at the point of despatch and can reduce the blended cost of your working capital stack as the transition debt amortises.

### Secondary, CEO

**Employee ownership and working capital: give your team the same EOMFM advantage you give retailers**

Your employees now own the business but the EOMFM model means they collectively extend 45 days of interest-free credit to thousands of UK indie retailers. Triver converts that float into immediate cash, improving the P&L and giving the Trust headroom to reinvest in the range.

### Tertiary, Finance Director

**Embedded credit scoring on the indie retailer book**

With 10,000+ SKUs going to indie retailers on EOMFM credit, the overdue tail is material. Synapto Recover layers credit scoring and automated follow-up onto the existing RBS facility, reducing DSO leakage without adding headcount.
