## PRQ preliminary findings

**Clean MoR confirmed in T&Cs**

T&Cs: 'No order accepted until the Company has accepted the order'; payment to Cotswold Fayre Ltd; Company owns liability for non-delivery/shortages.

**Lloyds Bank Commercial Finance ABL charge Oct 2024**

Outstanding charge registered 2024-10-22 with Lloyds Bank Commercial Finance Limited -- asset-based lending secured against trade debtors, confirming liquidity need.

**Group swung to loss FY2025**

Group loss after tax £514k (FY to March 2025) vs profit £93k prior year; wholesale division profit fell from £412k to £189k. Revenue up £0.9m to £27.2m. (Source: The Grocer, Dec 2025)

**2,000 indie food retailers on credit accounts**

Business model confirmed: 2,000 UK/IE food halls, delis, farm shops on credit accounts. T&Cs charge 8% above base on overdue balances plus agency referral fees.

**400 artisan producers paid via BACS**

Business model: 4,000 SKUs from 400 producers. No automated payout infrastructure evident from website or T&Cs -- manual BACS batch likely.

## Outreach angles

### Primary, Head of Finance

**Triver advance on the retailer debtor book**

Your 2,000-retailer credit book is generating the cash pressure that drove you to an ABL facility in October -- Triver can advance against those invoices the day you ship, converting the float into working capital without an additional revolving drawdown.

### Secondary, CEO

**Purpose-led supply chain: pay producers the day you order**

A B Corp with 400 artisan producers on 30-60 day BACS cycles is effectively subsidising retailer credit with producer credit. Synapto Flow pays producers on order confirmation, not month-end batch -- a genuine impact story aligned with your values.

### Tertiary, Commercial Director

**Embedded payments as a growth enabler for new producers**

Each new producer you onboard comes with a BACS setup and a 30-day payment lag. Embedding payment into the ordering workflow removes that friction, accelerates producer onboarding, and gives you a scalable treasury stack as you push toward double-digit growth.
