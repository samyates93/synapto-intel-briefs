## PRQ preliminary findings

**Merchant of Record - contracting party confirmed (Art 2.3)**

T&C Article 2.3: 'Language Reach may consider as a Client any person or entity that has placed an order with the Language Reach, unless said person or entity explicitly states that they are acting on the instructions, on behalf and at the expense of a third party.' LR is the named contracting party.

**Sub-contracting permitted without prejudice to liability (Art 4.3)**

T&C Article 4.3: 'Language Reach shall be entitled to hire third parties to carry out the order (in full or in part), without prejudice to the Language Reach responsibility for the confidential treatment and proper execution of the order.' Freelance linguists are sub-contractors; liability stays with LR.

**Exclusive liability to client retained (Art 8.1)**

T&C Article 8.1: 'Language Reach shall exclusively be liable to the Client for any loss or damage directly and demonstrably deriving from shortcomings attributable to the Language Reach.' Full liability chain intact - textbook MoR.

**Net-10 B2B payment terms (Art 6.5)**

T&C Article 6.5: 'Payment for products supplied or services rendered shall be due 10 calendar days after the invoice date.' B2B invoice-based collection confirmed.
