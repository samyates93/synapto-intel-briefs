## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Strong customer service reputation (4.76/5 on Reviews.co.uk from 18,288 reviews; 4 stars Trustpilot). Active Brand Partner acquisition via Carro (5+ new brands/week). Profitable with growing retained earnings. Scandiborn acquisition accelerated group to 10,000+ orders/month from 4,000 pre-acquisition. Well-defined MoR architecture confirmed in T&Cs.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

No automated payout rail for Brand Partners - with 5+ new partners onboarded weekly, manual BACs batch settlement is a scaling bottleneck. Director loan of £250k (interest-free, repayable on demand) from parent Trading Village Ltd is an off-balance-sheet demand liability. No dedicated finance function identified - Laura Davies appears to absorb this. Occasional Brand Partner dropship delays surfacing in reviews.

**🟡 Payment posture**

B2C card-at-checkout via Shopify Payments (Visa/MC/Amex). Consumer pays upfront. Brand Partners settle after dispatch - mechanism likely manual BACs batch. Float window estimated 30-60 days between consumer receipt and Brand Partner settlement. With hundreds of Brand Partners and 5+ new additions per week, the reconciliation surface is growing rapidly.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

Full PRQ already completed: Green (High confidence). T&Cs explicitly state: 'any sales and purchases you make in our Store are made directly with Scandibørn'. Brand Partners are downstream. Classic ICP 1 MoR architecture.
