## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Profitable, cash-strong membership body (£781k cash, £630k net assets). Established 1989 (35+ years). ~1,000 independent firms across 1,865 sites plus 100 associate suppliers. Confirmed Direct Debit subscription model with graded fee schedule. SAIFCharter provides governance link to FCA-authorised Golden Charter funeral plan trust, differentiating SAIF from smaller bodies. No bank debt.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Trade creditors spiked +923% in FY24 (£8k to £83k) - severe supplier payment timing issue despite strong cash reserves; signals internal payment process is not keeping pace. Other creditors (likely deferred subscription income) fell from £312k to £153k, suggesting subscription collection timing shifted. No separate Finance Director visible - CEO Terry Tennens and volunteer Treasurer Nasir Shabbir carry the financial management burden across 1,100+ annual billings.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Annual Direct Debit subscription collection confirmed via join-us page. Graded pricing (Grade 1 £433 to Grade 12 £2,634 per firm per year; associates £574/year). 1,865 sites (not just 1,000 firms) creates a complex billing roster. Associate suppliers may generate overrider/rebate income distributed back to member firms. SAIFCharter funeral plan flows are FCA-regulated and ring-fenced - separate from SAIF's subscription MoR.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

Trade-body MoR: SAIF collects subscriptions in its own name. Standard compatible structure. SAIFCharter Limited (05663828) is a separate governance entity for the Golden Charter funeral plan relationship - FCA-authorised, ring-fenced, outside SAIF's direct payment perimeter. Full T&C review required to confirm subscription MoR and assess rebate distribution flows.

## PRQ preliminary findings

**Trade-body MoR - Direct Debit subscription collection in own name**

SAIF collects annual subscriptions by Direct Debit directly from member firms and associate suppliers. Graded fee schedule confirmed on join-us page. Standard trade-body MoR pattern - SAIF is contracting and collecting entity.

**SAIFCharter / Golden Charter funeral plan flows are ring-fenced - separate from SAIF subscription MoR**

SAIF Charter Limited (05663828) is a separate legal entity. Golden Charter Trust is FCA-authorised and independent. Funeral plan purchase money flows into the trust, not through SAIF's subscription billing system. The PRQ Green is for the subscription MoR only.

**Associate supplier rebate distribution to member firms - flow direction and mechanism unclear**

Website references associate supplier programme; financial accounts show trade creditor spike which may relate to supplier payment obligations. Whether SAIF holds rebate funds and distributes to members (Synapto Flow use case) or whether this is a referral/commission arrangement needs confirmation.
