## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Largest UK funeral trade body with 3,800+ member firms conducting 470,000+ funerals annually. Sole body running CMA-compliant Funerals Market Order 2021 standards. NAFD Resolve is UK's only free funeral dispute resolution service. Strong 120-year brand (founded 1905). Sole access to NAFD Learning Hub, £5m professional indemnity, and FDM magazine.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

FY25 first-ever net loss (~£229k); net assets more than halved (£448k to £218k) in single year. Trade creditors tripled to £238k - supplier payment timing stretched. No Finance Director visible in leadership team. 55% headcount growth (11 to 17) in a loss year suggests cost-revenue mismatch. Two board president departures in 12 months. Secretary resigned mid-2025.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Subscription billing is the core income engine across three membership tiers (firm, supplier, overseas) plus Gold Partner Programme commercial income. Subs collected annually or periodically in own name (trade-body MoR). Deferred membership income likely represents the core float (other creditors £539k in 2024, fell to £121k in 2025). Pays suppliers (Gold Partners, CEDR, events vendors) via conventional bank transfer. No evidence of Stripe Connect or embedded payments.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

Trade-body MoR structure. NAFD collects membership subscriptions directly; members are B2B (funeral firms). No consumer-facing payment flow for main membership. However 'Perfect Choice reserve' in accounts hints at a pre-paid funeral plan product which may have FCA-regulated trust flows - verify separately. PRQ: AMBER (preliminary compatible, full T&C review required).
