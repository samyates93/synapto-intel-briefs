33-year track record with not one missed deadline claimed publicly. In-house employee model (no subcontracting) gives quality control edge over crowdsourced competitors. ISO/Cyber Essentials, GDPR, AQR and MRS accredited. Encrypted file upload platform (Global Lounge).

Cash at £11k is very tight. Net assets only £119 - barely positive after recovering from negative equity (£15.5k losses) in FY2024. Company reliant on RBS Invoice Finance facility for liquidity. B2B net-30 invoicing to NHS, corporate, legal, and academic clients. RBS Invoice Finance charge outstanding since March 2023 - active invoice advance facility. Trade debtors growing 40% YoY (£73k to £103k) while cash declining (£22k to £11k). Net-30 float is the primary working capital constraint.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**
