No marketplace or facilitator language. 110+ freelancers are contractors to TP Transcription, not platform sub-contractors in a marketplace sense.

## Payment flow

**NHS / Corporate / Government / Legal / University clients** (B2B, net-21 invoiced) → **TP Transcription + Synapto** → **Freelance transcribers and translators** (110+ freelancers, project-based pay, manual BACs)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Manual BACs to 110+ freelance transcribers/translators
- Beneficiary type: Freelance transcribers and translators
- Float window: Net-21 invoicing to B2B clients creates a 21-day float between service delivery and cash receipt. Transcribers need paying after project completion - timing gap is the operational pain.
