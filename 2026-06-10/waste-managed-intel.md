## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Rapid growth: revenue £11m (FY22) to £24.7m (FY23), 124% YoY. Secured future contracted revenue of £25.7m at Sep 2023. 36,000+ customers. 99% UK postcode coverage via sub-contractor network. B Corp and CIWMS certified. Now backed by Biffa - largest UK waste company.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Technically insolvent on a standalone basis (net current liabilities £8.2m at Sep 2023). Trade creditors growing 69% YoY against near-zero cash. Operating loss (£4.54m) driven by customer acquisition spend. Trustpilot score declining (4.2 to 3.9). Multiple founding team members departed on Biffa acquisition.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Consolidated B2B monthly invoicing to customers. Sub-contractor settlement cycle not disclosed but heavily evidenced by trade creditor surge. Cash at £416k vs creditors £4.18m indicates chronic settlement delay. Entirely reliant on parent group inter-company funding to bridge the gap.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

Green (Medium confidence). Consolidated invoice model confirmed. Full T&Cs not publicly available - service T&Cs gap is the confidence limiter. Now Biffa subsidiary adds layer of complexity to PRQ - full review required at both Waste Managed and group level.
