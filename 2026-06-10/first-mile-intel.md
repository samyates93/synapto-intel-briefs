## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Market leader in B2B zero-to-landfill waste for urban SMEs. 30,000+ business customers, 2,198 Trustpilot reviews at 4 stars, B Corp certified. 20% YoY revenue growth over a decade. Warren Equity Partners backing and acquisition-led consolidation strategy.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Trustpilot complaints centre on missed collections and billing problems. Group CFO departed Feb 2026 and seat appears vacant. Three director departures in 12 months alongside founder step-back introduces execution risk during a period of rapid inorganic growth.

**🟡 Payment posture**

B2B invoicing to 30,000 business customers (monthly cycle). 300+ carrier sub-contractors paid from those receipts - payout mechanism not publicly disclosed. Float window between customer receipt and carrier settlement is material at £50m scale.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

Green. T&Cs confirmed First Mile is the direct contracting party ('provides recycling and waste collection services to business Customers'). Not a marketplace or facilitator. MoR model confirmed at PRQ stage.

## Payment flow

**Business customer** (30,000+ UK businesses, invoiced monthly) → **First Mile + Synapto** → **Licensed carrier** (300+ sub-contracted waste collectors nationwide)

- Pays in: B2B
- Beneficiary type: Licensed waste carrier / collection sub-contractor
- Float window: B2B customer invoicing cycle (monthly) to carrier settlement creates a float window. At £50m revenue and 300+ carriers, the gap between customer payment receipt and carrier disbursement is a material working capital lever.
