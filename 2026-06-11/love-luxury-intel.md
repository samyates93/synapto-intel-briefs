## Payment flow

**Luxury buyer** (B2C; in-store Knightsbridge + Dubai + online) → **Love Luxury + Synapto** → **Consignor** (Seller of pre-owned designer item; receives net proceeds via bank transfer)

- Pays in: B2C
- Beneficiary type: Consignor (item owner)
- Float window: On commission sales: buyer payment received by Love Luxury; consignor paid net proceeds after sale. On outright purchases: Love Luxury funds the purchase from its own cash position, then sells. High-ticket average (£10k-£50k per Hermes/Rolex item) means even a few-day float on consignment settlements represents meaningful float.
