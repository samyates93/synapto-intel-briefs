## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Bootstrapped to profitability without external investment. Trustpilot 5 stars from 244 reviews. Distinctive brand with cult social following (168k Instagram). Frasers Group partnership validates brand quality at scale. Monaco presence adds international credibility.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

19-person team managing manual consignor payout reconciliation across 6+ locations. Director loans still outstanding (£121k). No dedicated finance function visible from filings or press. Commission-based revenue model structurally exposed to acquiring costs.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Merchant of record confirmed; buyer pays Sellier full item price; Sellier retains 30-50% commission and disburses net proceeds to consignor within 3 working days. Standard Stripe processor (no Connect). No split-payment infrastructure.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

## PRQ preliminary findings

**MoR confirmed via T&Cs**

T&Cs s7.1: consignor remains legal owner until sale. s8.1: Net Sale Value deducted of commission/fees paid to Sellier. Sellier acts as MoR on all transactions. Confirmed Green.

**No Stripe Connect**

Standard Stripe integration (not Stripe Connect). Sellier receives full payment; no split-payment or marketplace disbursement structure. Consistent with MoR model.

**Tiered commission 30-50%**

Commission tiering creates variable effective acquiring cost pressure depending on item value and commission tier. Lower-tier items (30%) face proportionally higher card cost drag.
