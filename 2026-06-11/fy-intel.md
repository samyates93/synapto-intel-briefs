## Payment flow

**Consumer** (B2C; UK, Europe, US; 1M+ customers) → **Fy! + Synapto** → **Fulfilment partner / supplier** (Prodigi (print production); boutique suppliers via Trouva)

- Pays in: B2C
- Beneficiary type: Print fulfillment partner (Prodigi) and boutique partners (Trouva)
- Float window: Short float window between customer payment and fulfilment cost; not material as print-on-demand fulfillment is cost-settled periodically, not per-transaction.
