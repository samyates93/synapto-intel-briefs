## Payment flow

**NHS Trusts, Courts, Councils and Central Government** (B2B, 30-45 day invoice terms) → **Dals + Synapto** → **Freelance Interpreters** (8,000 subcontractors, per-booking payout via DA Link platform)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Per-booking or periodic batch via DA Link platform
- Beneficiary type: Freelance interpreters and translators (classified as self-employed subcontractors; worker status disputed by Leigh Day group claim)
- Float window: NHS, courts and council clients pay on 30-45 day invoice terms. Interpreter payouts occur at booking completion or periodic batch. PE-backed structure means working capital management is a finance-level priority.
