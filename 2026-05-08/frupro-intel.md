## PRQ preliminary findings

**MoR confirmed in Reselling Services / FruPay**

[verify] T&Cs Reselling Services clause: FruPro accepts legal title to stock and acts as seller; pays sellers within 30 days. Standard marketplace transactions are buyer-seller direct (FruPro not in flow). The MoR is scoped to FruPay only, not all transactions.

**Finmid as incumbent payment infrastructure**

finmid powers FruPay via embedded finance API (4-week implementation). Finmid funds 48h payouts to suppliers against 30-day buyer terms. Reported 45% conversion uplift and 35% AOV increase post-launch. 23 countries.

**Debtor book growing fast, cash near-zero**

Trade debtors up 122% YoY to £487k at Aug 2024; cash down 83% to £48k. Fuel Ventures pre-seed £715k injected in tranches Jun-Sep 2024 provided the lifeline. Current runway post-round not disclosed.

**Dual co-founder exit July 2024**

William Hill (majority shareholder, 50-75% PSC, 130-year family produce background) and Kovthaman Murugaratman (CTO) both resigned within 5 days in July 2024. Hill fully exited as PSC by September 2024. No public explanation. Oliver Morley-Fletcher (b.1994) now leads as CEO.
