**Secondary**

You're running a 29-person operation that invoices some of the UK's largest estate-agent networks on monthly accounts. That's a quality debtors book - but monthly billing cycles create a predictable cash-flow dip between survey delivery and client payment.

We advance against confirmed invoices for businesses in exactly this position: the cash arrives when the survey is done, not when the client gets round to paying. It's not a loan against the business - it's funded by the invoices you've already earned.

Would it be useful to see what this looks like for Easy EPC's current billing volume? 20 minutes.
