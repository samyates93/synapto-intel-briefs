### Secondary, Co-founder

**Secondary**

Marcus,

The B2B billing side of Viewber - invoicing estate agents and asset managers per visit at scale - creates a receivables book that grows proportionally with the platform. At 150k+ visits a year that's a meaningful debtors position, and most estate-agent accounts settle monthly.

We can advance against confirmed Viewber invoices so the member payout cycle doesn't depend on when the estate agent processes their monthly statement. The estate agents are high-quality debtors; the cost of the advance is correspondingly low.

Happy to run through what this looks like for Viewber's current billing volume. 20 minutes?
