## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Profitable bootstrapped business with growing P&L reserves (GBP297k FY2025). Staff headcount up 63% in one year (11 to 18). Strong locum testimonials emphasising speed and personal service. NHS and private practice client base provides reliable B2B debtors.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Cash position very thin (GBP24k). Relies on invoice discounting facility (GBP173k Santander/Barclays) to bridge practice payment lag. Blog and content marketing stalled since 2022. No careers page - recruitment marketing gap.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Invoices NHS and private practices on B2B terms. Pays locums within a week of work. Invoice discounting facility (GBP173k, secured by fixed and floating charge on all assets) actively used to fund the float between billing and payout. Santander refinance Oct 2024 (replaced Barclays charges from 2017-2018).

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

PRQ Green confirmed. Employment business (EAA 1973 s13(3)). T&Cs reference 'standard Terms of Business' for each placement. No PayFac/marketplace language. No Stripe Connect. AppLocum is principal in the payment flow.

## Payment flow

**NHS / Private Practice** (B2B invoice) → **AppLocum + Synapto** → **Locum Clinicians** (GPs, nurses, ANPs, HCAs)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Within 1 week of shift completion
- Beneficiary type: Locum clinicians (GPs, nurses, ANPs, HCAs)
- Float window: NHS and private practice payment terms (typically 30-60 days) vs locum pay within one week = 3-7 week float window. Currently funded by Barclays/Santander invoice discounting (GBP173k drawn).
