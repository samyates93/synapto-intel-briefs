## Payment flow

**Tier-1 clients** (HS2, Alstom, Babcock, Network Rail, utilities) → **Carrington West + Synapto** → **Contractors** (PAYE engineers and technical workers)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Weekly PAYE payroll to 70+ technical consultants
- Beneficiary type: Engineers and technical contractors (PAYE weekly payroll)
- Float window: Clients contracted to pay within 7 days of weekly invoice; contractors paid weekly. In practice, Tier-1 contractors (HS2, Alstom, Babcock) likely settle 30-60 days. RBS Invoice Finance facility active to bridge this gap.
