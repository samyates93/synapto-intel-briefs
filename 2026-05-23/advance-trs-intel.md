**Framework clients** (Alstom, Babcock, Network Rail, Heathrow, HS2) → **Advance TRS + Synapto** → **Contractors / umbrella companies** (Rail and infrastructure specialists via umbrella PAYE)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Weekly settlement to umbrella payroll companies; placement fees retained
- Beneficiary type: Rail and infrastructure contractors (via umbrella PAYE or PSC; Umbrella PAYE only permitted per contractor ads)
