## Payment flow

**NHS Trust / Private CAMHS Provider** (B2B, HTE TWS III framework, net-30/45) → **CAMHS Professionals + Synapto** → **CAMHS Locum Clinicians** (PAYE + umbrella, bi-weekly)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Yes - bi-weekly PAYE and umbrella disbursements
- Beneficiary type: CAMHS locum clinicians (nurses, psychologists, psychiatrists, social workers, allied health) on PAYE and umbrella contracts
- Float window: NHS Trusts pay net-30 to net-45 days under framework terms; CAMHS pays locums bi-weekly. Structural float gap of 2-6 weeks per placement cycle, currently funded by RBS Invoice Finance facility.
