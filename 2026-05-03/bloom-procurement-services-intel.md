## Payment flow

**Public Sector Client** (B2B invoice (HMRC, Home Office, Cabinet Office etc.)) → **Bloom Procurement Services + Synapto** → **SME Supplier** (UK professional services SME)

- Pays in: B2B
- Payouts: Weekly BACS runs, processed Friday, clearing following Tuesday. Average 27.5 days from invoice submission.
- Beneficiary type: SME professional services suppliers (380+ subcategories, 73% of projects awarded to SMEs)
- Float window: Public sector clients pay Bloom on invoice (typically 30 days). Bloom pays SME suppliers weekly via BACS (average 27.5 days). Float window up to 30+ days on Bloom balance sheet. At £273m annual turnover, float represents c.£22m average receivables book at any point.
