## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

98% English LA framework coverage. APSCo and REC accredited. Multi-brand group (Diverse Recruitment Group) covering social work, health and veterinary. Tommy Love as sole director with strong operational continuity since 2014. Government-backed debtor book is high-quality collateral for working capital finance.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Invoice finance costs (£545k) now exceed operating profit (£397k), producing a net loss. Cash at £19k: operationally dependent on invoice finance facility with zero cash buffer. Revenue slightly declining (minus 1.07% FY2024). Gross margin compressed from 8.5% to 7.7%. Government price caps on agency social workers from Oct 2025 add further margin pressure. Accounts filed three months late in Dec 2025.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Social Personnel collects from LA clients on 30-45 day invoice terms and pays social workers weekly via in-house PAYE payroll plus Ltd company umbrella arrangements. The float gap is bridged entirely by invoice finance (ABN Amro and Skipton Business Finance combined). Total interest cost: £545,829 per annum (FY2024).

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

Employment business model confirmed. Social Personnel acts as principal employer, collecting from LAs and paying workers. AMBER: preliminary compatible with Synapto Advance and Flow. No marketplace or introduction-only language. No Stripe Connect signals found. Full PRQ required. Ltd company umbrella payroll adds complexity to the payout rail review.
