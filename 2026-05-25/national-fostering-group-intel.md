## Payment flow

**Payer** () → **National Fostering Group + Synapto** → **Beneficiary** ()

- Pays in: B2B
- Beneficiary type: Self-employed foster carers (3,000+)
- Float window: LA placement fees paid on net-30 to net-45 invoice terms; NFG pays carers weekly. Float window 30-45 days. At 3,000 carers x ~£500/week average, weekly carer payroll is approximately £1.5m. This creates a persistent working-capital draw that must be funded out of operating cash or a credit facility.
