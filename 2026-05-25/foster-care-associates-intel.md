## Payment flow

**Payer** () → **Foster Care Associates + Synapto** → **Beneficiary** ()

- Pays in: B2B
- Beneficiary type: Self-employed foster carers (1,400+)
- Float window: LA placement fees paid on net-30 to net-45 invoice terms; FCA pays carers bi-weekly. At 1,400 carers at avg £500/week, bi-weekly carer payroll is ~£700k per cycle. Float gap 30-45 days. Across the Polaris fostering brands (FCA + ISP + Fosterplus), this scales to a group-level working-capital draw of several million per cycle.
