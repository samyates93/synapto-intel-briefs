## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

26 years in the supply teacher market. Crown Commercial Services (CCS) framework approved - direct supply route to every English school without tender. National Tutoring Programme approved partner (2 years running). REC accredited, ISO 9001/14001. Multi-award Best Companies 2023. International teacher pipeline (CA/AU/NZ/IE). 23+ branch network across UK.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Extremely thin net assets (£11.8k as at Oct 2024). Cash on hand £36k against £20.5m creditors - entirely dependent on debtor conversion speed. Trade debtors grew 30% YoY to £20.4m. Trustpilot (45 reviews, mixed): transparency complaints on PAYE vs umbrella deductions, DBS processing delays. No dedicated finance/payments leadership role publicly visible.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Schools and MATs pay Connex on 30-60 day invoice terms (B2B). Connex pays 13k+ teachers weekly via PAYE. The working capital gap between payroll outflow and debtor collection is the core Liquidity signal. Trade debtors of £20.4m with cash of £36k means group treasury or overdraft facilities must bridge every weekly payroll cycle.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

PRQ: Green confirmed. Connex is the employer of record - not a marketplace or facilitator. PAYE payroll confirmed by website, Trustpilot reviews, and accounts structure. Schools invoice B2B, teachers employed directly by Connex entity. No MoR or PayFac risk identified.
