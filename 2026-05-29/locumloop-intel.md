# LocumLoop

**PRQ: Green** (High) · ICP B · **Liquidity wedge** · locumloop.com

---

## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Largest UK dental nurse locum platform by own claim; 90%+ job fill rate; 50% of jobs filled within 15 minutes; transparent pay structure; named clients include Colosseum Dental and Clyde Munro.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

3 employees, micro-entity accounts, net liabilities (£15,114) at March 2025; no external funding; Airlocum competitor claims 1,900+ practices and backing from Bupa and MyDentist; 7-30 day nurse pay window is slower than gig-economy alternatives.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Employment business / MoR shape: LocumLoop collects full daily rate from practices (nurse pay plus £30 platform fee) and disburses to nurses within 7-30 days. Float window sits on the company balance sheet - currently funded by director loans approaching repayment.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

PRQ: AMBER, preliminary compatible. MoR / employment business shape confirmed; 'marketplace' language present but payment responsibility clearly sits with LocumLoop. Full T&C review required before commercial engagement.

## PRQ preliminary findings

**MoR / Employment business shape**

FAQ states 'We charge a daily rate to the practice and take none from your salary' and nurses are 'paid directly into your bank account within 7-30 days'. LocumLoop sets the nurse rate and pays direct - this is employment business / MoR shape, not a marketplace connector.

**Marketplace language present alongside MoR structure**

Website uses 'connecting dental nurses with dental practices' and 'platform' terminology. However, the payment structure (LocumLoop collects and disburses) clearly indicates employment business responsibility, not mere introduction.

**No Stripe Connect signals; payment mechanism not disclosed**

Platform states 'pay both locumloop and locums automatically after a successful job' but does not specify the payment processor. No Stripe Connect references found. Preliminary compatible.
