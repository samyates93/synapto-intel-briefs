# LaundryRepublic

**PRQ: Green** (High) · ICP A · **Margin wedge** · laundryrepublic.com

> UK dry-cleaning and laundry collection-delivery platform operating a locker/concierge handover network across hundreds of London buildings, collecting card payment in own name and sub-contracting cleaning operations.

---

## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

- 17-year business, award-winning London premium laundry brand (Accelerate 250, LCN Awards, South London Business)
- Trustpilot 5 stars, 1,227 reviews - strong NPS and repeat loyalty
- Dual market: 7,000+ residential customers + commercial division (hotels, restaurants, spas, gyms, property managers)
- Locker and concierge integration across hundreds of London buildings - high-frequency repeat card transactions
- Sustainability differentiator: NASA ozone tech, Miele/Sensene systems, electric fleet, 40% plastic reduction
- Intangible assets £115k - proprietary booking/tracking platform
- Losses dramatically reduced FY23-FY24 (-£400k to -£180k) - business moving towards breakeven

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

- Cash stress: £45k cash (halved YoY) vs £201k current creditors - tight working capital
- Trade creditors +136% YoY (£65k to £153k) - sub-contractor payment stretched; potential friction in supply chain
- Other debtors +246% YoY (£74k to £255k) - unexplained; possible B2B invoice collection challenge
- Still loss-making (accumulated losses -£180k) despite 17 years of operation
- HSBC fixed + floating charge active since 2020 - existing leverage against assets
- No finance director identified; founders managing all financial operations

**🟡 Payment posture**

Inbound: consumer card, PayPal, Direct Debit (website confirms all three). B2B commercial clients likely on invoice terms given other debtor surge. Outbound: sub-contracted cleaning facilities and logistics on trade credit terms (£153k owed at FY24 year end). Electric van fleet under finance lease (£70k current portion). HSBC bank loan £67k short-term + £56k long-term.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

T&Cs explicitly state LaundryRepublic contracts directly with customers, charges card in its own name, and sub-contracts cleaning as a service provider - not a marketplace. No PayFac language, no Stripe Connect indicators. Green PRQ posture. Full PRQ required before commercial engagement.
