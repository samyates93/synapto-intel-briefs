## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

- Bootstrapped to £1.5m-£2m revenue in 10 years with 11 employees and 250 VAs - exceptional capital efficiency
- 95% of clients on Direct Debit - predictable, low-churn subscription revenue
- 4.97/5 on Reviews.io (100% verified), Glassdoor 4.7/5, 94% recommend as employer
- Multiple award finalist: 2024 British Business Awards, 2024 Allica Bank Great British Entrepreneur Awards
- Profitable: retained earnings £68,722 (FY25) vs £31,054 (FY24); cash up from £206k to £316k
- 10-year milestone celebrated in 2024-2025; 2026 rebrand signals next growth phase
- UK-only VA base (no offshore outsourcing) is a strong differentiator

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

- No dedicated finance function - founders handle all back-office
- Small team (11 employees) managing 250 VAs creates operational stretch as platform scales
- No external funding: growth capped by cashflow; Synapto Advance directly addresses this
- Trustpilot not actively managed (company switched to Reviews.io)
- No current job postings visible - either lean or not advertising

**🟡 Payment posture**

Inbound: monthly subscription billing via Direct Debit (95% of clients), card and bank transfer. Plans £310-£2,700/month. Auto-renew annually. Outbound: VA payments to 250 self-employed VAs from pooled subscription revenue (mechanism not publicly disclosed, likely BACS batch). Float window ~2-4 weeks on £87k/month VA obligations.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

T&Cs confirm Virtalent Ltd as sole contracting party with both clients ('Virtalent Ltd provides Virtual Assistant services') and VAs ('We are responsible for the payment of a Virtual Assistant's fees'). All payments directed to Virtalent's bank account. No marketplace/facilitator language. No PayFac disclaimers. Green PRQ posture confirmed by prior PRQ skill run.
