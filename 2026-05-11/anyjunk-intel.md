## At a glance

**🟢 Strengths**

Profitable bootstrapped operator: £3.25m operating profit on £20.4m revenue in FY2024, entirely self-funded with no external investors. Strong B2B commercial partnerships (B&Q, Wickes, Co-op, HSS Hire). Confirmed MoR position with clean T&Cs. 500+ carrier network across UK. ISO 9001/14001, Living Wage employer, EcoVadis Silver. Service Partner app (iOS + Android) for carrier job management.

**🔴 Weaknesses & pain points**

Software services revenue collapsed 82% year-on-year (£5.3m to £985k), implying a failed or wound-down B2B SaaS/licensing arm. Staff headcount fell from 51 to 44. Trade debtors at 83 days average (£4.66m) suggest slow-paying B2B accounts. Trustpilot 4.0 stars vs Clearabee at 5.0 and Litta at 4.9. No identifiable CFO. No public press in 18+ months.

**🟡 Payment posture**

Customers pay AnyJunk at time of collection (B2C prepay) or on invoice terms (B2B accounts). AnyJunk pays partner carriers via batch bank transfers; precise timing not disclosed but trade creditors imply ~38-day payment lag. Service Partner app exists for job management but no native payment rail identified.

**🟡 PRQ / PayFac signal**

PRQ confirmed Green (Very High confidence) from discovery routine. T&Cs explicitly confirm MoR: AnyJunk contracts with both customer and carrier as principal. No PayFac facilitator language. No Stripe Connect evidence. Full PRQ verified.

## PRQ preliminary findings

**MoR language confirmed in T&Cs**

T&Cs section 1: 'a contract shall be created between us on your acceptance of our quote.' Section 3: 'Payment must be made before or at the time of the clearance.' AnyJunk acts as principal, not agent. No facilitator or introduces language found in the T&Cs reviewed during discovery.

**Carrier relationship is subcontracted dispatch, not platform agency**

AnyJunk dispatches licensed waste carriers who operate under the AnyJunk brand on each job. AnyJunk is the counterparty to the customer; the carrier is a supply-chain subcontractor. This is consistent with the ISV/MoR model and does not exhibit PayFac characteristics.

**No Stripe Connect or PayFac signals identified**

No mention of Stripe Connect, marketplace split payments, or facilitator infrastructure found on the website, in press, or in filed accounts. Payment mechanism: customer pays AnyJunk by card or invoice; AnyJunk pays carrier by bank transfer.
