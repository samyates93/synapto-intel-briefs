## Payment flow

**Payer** () → **Heat Geek Installations + Synapto** → **Beneficiary** ()

- Pays in: B2C
- Beneficiary type: Independent verified heat pump installers (MCS-certified subcontractors)
- Float window: Heat Geek deducts the BUS grant (currently £7,500-£9,000 per install) from the homeowner invoice upfront, then claims reimbursement from Ofgem after MCS audit. Ofgem processing typically takes 4-8 weeks. Other debtors of £252,928 at Mar 2025 likely represent outstanding BUS grant receivables. As ZeroDisrupt drives higher installation volumes, this float grows proportionally.
