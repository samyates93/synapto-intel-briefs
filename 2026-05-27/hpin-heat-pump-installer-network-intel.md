## Payment flow

**Payer** () → **HPIN (Heat Pump Installer Network) + Synapto** → **Beneficiary** ()

- Pays in: B2C
- Beneficiary type: Independent MCS-certified heat pump installers on Supply of Service contracts
- Float window: Customer pays CB Heating/HPIN; CB Heating applies for BUS grant (£7,500-£9,000) from Ofgem on their behalf; installer paid post-completion audit. Float window exists (weeks between install completion and Ofgem reimbursement) but EDF balance sheet absorbs this without the working capital pressure a standalone operator would face.
